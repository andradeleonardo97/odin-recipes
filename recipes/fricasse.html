Chicken Fricasse

This recipe is very pratical because it is made with help of a blender. Combining juicy pieces of chicken, a tasty cream and a special touch of ingredients such as corn and cream cheese, this dish is an explosion of flavors.

The preparation of fricassÃª involves cooking shredded chicken, which is then mixed with a delicious white sauce with corn and cream cheese, creating a creamy and irresistible mixture. This combination is placed in a refractory, covered with cheese and straw potatoes and placed in the oven until browned, ensuring a golden crust on top.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Cream cheese
  • Green corn
  • Cream of milk
  • Water
  • Olive
  • Straw potatoes

Preparation

  1. Cooker the chicken breast
  2. Mix green corn and cream of milk in the blender
  3. After the chicken breast already cooked its time to shred and saute
  4. Mix the delicious cream of green corn with the chicken breasts sauted
  5. Put everything in a container and put a little of cheese on the top
  6. After everything does its time to take in oven until see a little golden crust
  7. Finished!!! Ready to serve.
