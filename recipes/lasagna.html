Chicken Lasagna

Chicken and cheese lasagna is an irresistible version of this classic dish, combining the flavor of succulent chicken with the creaminess of melted cheese in delicious layers.

Prepare this recipe and savor each layer of comfort food, bringing a touch of warmth to your meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 400 g sliced ham
  • 1 pot of cream cheese
  • 2 glasses of milk
  • 500 g sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 1 medium package of lasagna pasta (directly from the oven, without prior cooking)
  • 1 box of cream
  • 3 spoons of butter

Preparation

  1. In a pan, make a homogeneous cream with 2 tablespoons of flour and 2 tablespoons of butter (reserve 1 tablespoon of butter).
  2. Add the milk, 1 chicken broth and stir constantly.
  3. Remove from heat and add the cream, set aside.
  4. Cook the chicken breast in water (without oil), once cooked, shred it.
  5. Chop the onion into small pieces, place in a pan and brown with butter.
  6. Add the chicken and chicken broth, stirring constantly until the chicken is completely golden..
  7. In a refractory, place 2 ladles to soak.
  8. Make the base with lasagna dough, cover with 1 layer of ham, 1 layer of cheese and 1 layer of chicken (in that order).
  9. Over the chicken, place 1 layer of cream cheese and 2 ladles of sauce.
  10. Cover the cottage cheese with 1 layer of ham, 1 layer of cheese and 1 layer of pasta, add sauce.
  11. Repeat this process until there is about 2.5 cm left to reach the edge of the refractory.
  12. To finish, cover the lasagna with lots of cheese, cream cheese and sauce..
  13. Bake for approximately 20 minutes over low heat.
