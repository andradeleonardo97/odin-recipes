Roast Chicken

If you are looking for a recipe that is easy to prepare and at the same time leaves your home with that irresistible aroma of delicious food, whole roast chicken is the ideal choice!

With just a few ingredients and a simple step-by-step guide, you will surprise everyone with a succulent dish full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • salt
  • rosemary and basil or dehydrated fine herbs
  • margarine or salted butter
  • 1 large onion, peeled whole
  • Lemon

Preparation

  1. Take the chicken, remove the giblets.
  2. Wash it inside and out with water and lemon, drain.
  3. Dry with a cloth napkin inside and outside, then salt inside and outside, do the same with margarine or butter.
  4. Place in a baking dish, add the whole onion and the sprigs of rosemary and basil inside the Seara chicken with a cube of margarine.
  5. Bake in a medium oven until it starts to brown for about 40 minutes, turn the oven up to maximum to brown and become crispy.
